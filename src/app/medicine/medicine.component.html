Medicine: Agrogenta 11

Formulation: Gentamicin (as sulphate) 110 mg, excipients q.s. ad 1 mL.

Indications: Treatment and prevention of infections caused by microorganisms that are sensitive to gentamicin (of the genitourinary, respiratory and gastrointestinal systems). Also useful in cases of mastitis, metritis, posoperatory and cutaneous infections, septicemias, among others.

Dosage & Administration: Cattle, horse, sheep, swine, camelid: 1 mL/27.5 kg of b.w.; young animals, dogs and cats: 1 mL/14 kg; poultry: 0.1 mL/1.4 kg; each 24 hours for 3 to 5 consecutive days subcutaneous or intramuscular route. Intrauterine or intra-mammary route: cows: 2 mL diluted in 20 mL of physiological saline solution for 3 to 5 days. Mares: 20 mL diluted in 200 - 500 mL of physiological saline solution for 3 to 5 days (only intrauterine route).

Medicine: Amoxigentin

Formulation: Amoxicillin (as trihydrate) 150 mg, Gentamycin (as sulphate) 40 mg, excipients q.s.ad 1 mL.

Indications: For the treatment of enteritis (produced by E. coli, Salmonella spp.,Clostridium spp.), infections caused by streptococcus spp., such as arthritis and meningitis, ear necrosis, urinary tract infections (nephritis, urethritis, cystitis, etc.), respiratory diseases (pneumonia, bronchitis, etc.), systemic infection and prevention of secondary bacterial infections.

Dosage & Administration: Single dose: 1 mL/10 Kg a day during 3 days or at the discretion of the veterinarian. Deep intramuscular route.

Medicine: Aureomycin

Formulation: Each 100 g contains: Chlortetracycline 20 g; excipients q.s. ad 100 g.

Indications: For poultry, the treatment of respiratory and digestive diseases such as CRD, mycoplasmosis, colibacillosis, among others.

Dosage & Administration: Poultry: 10-60 mg of chlortetracycline/kg of live weight. Promoter: 250-500 g of Aureomycin® G200 Px (50-100 ppm of chlortetracycline) per MT continuously. Preventive: 500 g-1 kg of Aureomycin® G200 Px (100-200 ppm of chlortetracycline) per MT for 14 days and/or during the risk period. Therapeutic: 1-3 kg of Aureomycin® G200 Px (200-600 ppm of chlortetracycline) for 7 days. Pigs: : 5-20 mg of chlortetracycline/kg of live weight. Promoter: 250-300 g of Aureomycin® G200 Px (50-60 ppm of chlortetracycline) per MT continuously. Preventive: 500 g-1 kg of Aureomycin® G200 Px (100-200 ppm of chlortetracycline) per MT for 14 days and/or during the risk period. Therapeutic: 1-2 kg of Aureomycin® G200 Px (200-400 ppm of chlortetracycline) for 7 days.

Medicine: Cefa-Milk

Formulation: Cephalexin monohydrate (base) 20 mg; excipients q.s. ad. 1mL.

Indications: For the treatment of mastitis in cattle, sheep and goats in milk production, caused by germs that are sensitive to cephalexin. It must be used at the first signs of inflammation or at the first indication of any alteration in the milk. The exclusive Duo Cap® system offers the possibility of the a deep application or administration near the nipple sphincter.

Dosage & Administration: Cattle: 1 syringe/ affected quarter every 12 - 24 hours during 2 days or until complete recovery; sheep and goats: 1/2 syringe /affected quarter every 12 - 24 hours during 2 days or until complete recovery. Apply through intrammamary infusion.
